Banglish features

Some top feature we are going to describe in here. more features are in there in the app. please installl the app to know more features in Banglish

Vowel letter

For vowel litters we will provide customers Shorborno. If they are able to press the right letter, they will get 1 point otherwise fail

Consonant letters

In the same way for pressing consonant letters, they will get Banjonborno. If they are able to press the right letter, they will get 1 point otherwise fail

Number system

Here we will provide Bangla number system to recognize numbers in Bangla.it will help to learn about the number and more

Words system

After pressing in any words it will return the right pronunciation of the word and also tell the Bangla meaning of the word
